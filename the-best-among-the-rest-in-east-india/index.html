GeeCab: The Best Among the Rest in East India

In the rapidly developing regions of East India, finding a dependable partner for safe, efficient and long-lasting electrical wiring is crucial. From powering smart homes in Bhubaneswar to supporting industrial hubs in Ranchi and ensuring seamless connectivity in Guwahati - GeeCab stands out as "The Best Among the Rest."

A Flagship Brand in Electrical Excellence

GeeCab, the flagship brand of Gananayak Cable Industries, a recognized leader in the wires and cable manufacturing for over two decades. Headquartered in Kolkata (West Bengal), we specialize in delivering high-performance, safety-first electrical solutions for residential, commercial and industrial applications.

From the energy demands of urban metros like Kolkata to the humid coastlines of Odisha and the rugged terrains of Assam, our products are engineered to perform in every condition.

Engineered for the East: Why GeeCab Stands Out?

Built for Performance: Every wire and cable we produce is crafted using high-grade copper and aluminium, ensuring 100% conductivity, superior insulation and fire resistance. Whether you're dealing with high-load industrial operations or residential setups, our products are built to last-even in the challenging climates of Assam or the humid conditions of coastal Odisha.

Certified & Reliable: Our manufacturing follows ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 14001:2015 systems. This guarantees that every GeeCab product meets international safety and quality standards, providing peace of mind to builders and engineers across Eastern India.

Our Wide Product Range - Tailored for Every Need

GeeCab offers an extensive selection of wires and cables, ensuring that every sector-residential, commercial and industrial is covered with precision-engineered solutions:

Copper Wires: Superior conductivity and flexibility for critical wiring needs

Aluminium Cables: Lightweight and cost-effective for large-scale power distribution

Single & Multicore Cables: Versatile solutions for residential and commercial setups

CCTV & Co-Axial Cables: Uninterrupted signal quality for surveillance and media

PVC Submersible Cables: Built to perform under extreme water and pressure conditions

CAT-5 Communication Cables: High-speed, stable data and internet transmission


Each product is backed by advanced manufacturing processes and rigorous testing to ensure unmatched performance and longevity.
