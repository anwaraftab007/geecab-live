Gananayak Cable Industries: Delivering Innovation and Quality Across Our Brand Portfolio

At Gananayak Cable Industries, we have built a strong reputation for excellence, with GeeCab standing out as one of our most trusted and recognized brands in the cable manufacturing sector. Known for quality, reliability and cutting-edge technology, the GeeCab has become synonymous with innovation in electrical and communication cable solutions.

We offer a comprehensive portfolio of industry-specific brands, including high-performance wires & cables, durable PVC conduit and flexible pipes, precision electric meters, protective casings, robust enclosures (gang/nano and MCB boxes), extension cords, and premium PVC electrical tape—delivering reliable, customized electrical solutions for diverse applications.
