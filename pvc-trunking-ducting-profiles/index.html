Available Sizes of PVC Trunking Profiles

Understanding that every project has unique space and wiring demands, GeeCab offers its trunking profiles in multiple standard sizes. Whether youâ€™re working in a constrained environment or managing a larger cable load, there's a profile designed for optimal fit and performance:

  • 25x12 mm: Perfect for general electrical installations in residential and office spaces. This size is frequently used for enclosing light switch wiring, fan connections and general cable management in visible or concealed wiring setups.

  • 20x12 mm: Optimized for low-voltage wiring systems or areas with minimal space for enclosures. Its narrow profile is especially useful behind wooden panelling, partition walls or furniture-based routing where discretion is key.

Q1. What makes GeeCab trunking profiles better than ordinary PVC casings?

Q2. Can your casing be painted or customized for specific interiors?

Yes, the outer surface of our PVC casing supports light sanding and painting. This makes it ideal for modern interiors where aesthetics matter. It can be adapted to match wall colours or design themes.

Q3. How long does your PVC casing last in typical indoor or outdoor conditions?

When installed correctly, our casing can last over a decade without warping, cracking or colour fading. It maintains structural integrity in both indoor and outdoor conditions, including high humidity, heat and exposure to direct sunlight.

Cable capacity depends on the profile size and wire thickness. For example, a 32x12 mm profile can comfortably handle multiple 1.5 sq. mm or 2.5 sq. mm wires used in lighting and power circuits. For larger wire loads, parallel installations may be recommended.
