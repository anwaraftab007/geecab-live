Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. What sets GeeCab apart from other manufacturers in the electrical cable space?

Decades of field experience, continuous product innovation and a deep commitment to quality have made GeeCab a preferred choice across sectors. Every cable undergoes comprehensive testing for insulation, conductivity and tensile strength, ensuring unmatched safety and reliability on-site.

Q2. Do your wires and cables meet national and international safety standards?

Yes, all products are manufactured under ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 14001:2015 certified systems. Compliance with BIS standards and rigorous internal quality protocols ensures that each unit performs safely under real-world conditions.

Q3. Do you offer custom cable lengths or packaging for bulk buyers?

We do. Based on your project or industrial requirement, we provide custom lengths, coil sizes and packaging options to ensure ease of transport, storage and installation. Custom labelling is also available for OEM partners.

Q4. What is the expected lifespan of your cables in normal conditions?

When installed and maintained properly, our cables can last over 20 years depending on application, load conditions and environmental factors. The use of high-quality insulation and conductors ensures long-term reliability without frequent replacements.

Q5. Are your cables suitable for use in high-moisture environments like submersible pumps?

Yes, our PVC submersible cables are engineered specifically for wet and submerged environments. They maintain conductivity, resist water ingress and prevent short circuits, making them ideal for agricultural, industrial and groundwater applications.
