When it comes to structured electrical setups in residential, commercial or industrial environments, GeeCab Gang Boxes offer the perfect combination of durability, safety and versatility. From clean design to uncompromised quality, we ensure every unit is crafted to meet the highest standards.

Whether you’re installing switches and sockets in a new construction project or upgrading an existing system, GeeCab Gang Boxes provide the strong foundation you need to organize your electrical components efficiently and safely.

Engineered for Reliability. Made for All Environments

GeeCab Gang Boxes are manufactured under stringent ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 14001:2015 certified systems. Every unit undergoes strict quality control and environmental checks to ensure it meets the highest standards. Crafted using premium-grade materials, our gang boxes are rust-resistant, heat-tolerant and capable of withstanding demanding conditions—indoors or outdoors.

With enhanced protection against short circuits, electrical surges and long-term wear, GeeCab Gang Boxes are engineered to keep you and your property safe while maintaining consistent performance.

Why Choose GeeCab Gang Boxes?

As a trusted gang box manufacturer in India, GeeCab blends cutting-edge design with long-standing technical expertise to provide a solution that not only performs but endures. Here's what makes our gang boxes the preferred choice:

  • Rugged Construction: Made from high-quality materials like powder-coated metal or impact-resistant PVC, GeeCab Gang Boxes are designed to withstand mechanical stress and harsh conditions.

  • Modular Design: Flexible design configurations with 1 Modular, 2 Modular, 3 Modular, 4 Modular, 5 Modular, 6 Modular, 7 Modular, 8 Modular and multi-gang options—ideal for both new installations and retrofits.

  • Flame-Resistant Design: Prioritizing safety, our boxes offer superior resistance to heat and fire, reducing the risk of electrical hazards.

  • Easy Installation: With precision-cut knockouts, adjustable mounting options, and pre-punched holes, installation becomes quicker and hassle-free for electricians and installers.

  • Corrosion & Moisture Resistant: Designed for indoor and outdoor use, our gang boxes are protected against rust and moisture—making them ideal for all weather conditions.

Applications Across All Segments

GeeCab Gang Boxes are suitable for a wide range of electrical installations:

  • Home Installations: Seamlessly integrate your switches, sockets and electrical fittings with clean and safe gang boxes designed to match modern aesthetics.

  • Offices and Workspaces: Enable organized cabling and secure electrical control in office desks, server rooms or meeting areas.

  • Industrial and Commercial Projects: Built to handle high-load environments such as factories, workshops, warehouses, and retail spaces where durability and safety are non-negotiable.
