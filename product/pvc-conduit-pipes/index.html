Engineered for Safety, Built for Performance

When safety, durability and efficiency are your priorities, trust GeeCab PVC Conduit Pipes, a premium product by Gananayak Cable Industries. With over two decades of expertise in the electrical industry, GeeCab offers robust, high-performance conduit solutions for residential, commercial and industrial wiring applications.


Engineered for Safety. Designed for Performance

Manufactured under ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 14001:2015 certified processes, GeeCab conduit pipes are produced using high-quality, fire-retardant virgin PVC. Our pipes are built to withstand extreme temperatures, moisture, corrosion and mechanical stress—making them the ideal solution for long-lasting cable management in diverse environments.

Whether you’re wiring a new home, setting up an office, or managing a large-scale industrial project, GeeCab delivers reliable conduit systems that keep your electrical infrastructure protected and organized.

Why Professionals Trust GeeCab PVC Conduit Pipes?

GeeCab combines time-tested engineering with modern manufacturing techniques to deliver conduit systems that meet the evolving needs of the electrical industry. Here's why professionals and installers trust GeeCab for their conduit solutions:

  • Superior Material Quality: Crafted from virgin PVC compounds, our pipes offer excellent insulation, rigidity and fire resistance—ideal for long-term installations.

  • Fire Safety Compliance: Flame-retardant and self-extinguishing, helping prevent the spread of fire in critical situations.

  • Corrosion-Resistant: Unlike metal conduits, our PVC pipes remain unaffected by rust or oxidation, even in damp environments.

  • Smooth Inner Surface: Ensures easy pulling and smooth passage of wires, minimizing chances of insulation damage during installation.

  • UV & Weather Resistant: Suitable for indoor and outdoor use, our pipes perform reliably under varying environmental conditions.

  • Easy Installation: Lightweight, flexible and easy to cut or bend with standard tools—saving time and labour costs.


GeeCab combines precision engineering with advanced manufacturing to meet the evolving needs of modern electrical infrastructure. As a leading PVC Electric Tape manufacturer in India, we understand the complexities of insulation and wire management, allowing us to design conduit systems that integrate seamlessly with wider electrical solutions.

Supporting India’s Growth, One Project at a Time

Produced under the Make in India initiative, GeeCab conduit pipes reflect the strength of Indian engineering and eco-conscious manufacturing. With modern facilities and a commitment to innovation, we are equipped to handle small-scale orders and large infrastructure projects with equal efficiency.

Applications Across Multiple Industries

GeeCab PVC conduit pipes are widely used in residential buildings, commercial complexes, factories and infrastructure projects. From concealed wiring in false ceilings to surface-mounted installations in workshops, our products provide secure routing and protection for all types of electrical cabling systems.

Commitment to Sustainability

GeeCab is committed to sustainable manufacturing. Our products are recyclable and produced using processes that minimize environmental impact. By reducing waste and conserving resources, we help build a safer, greener future without compromising on product quality.
