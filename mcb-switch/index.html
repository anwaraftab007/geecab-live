Complete Product Range for All Protection Needs

  • Single Pole MCBs (C Series): Engineered for residential and light commercial applications, our Single Pole MCBs are available in 6A, 10A, 16A, 20A, 25A and 32A ratings. Designed with C-curve tripping characteristics, these breakers respond efficiently to both overload and short circuit conditions without unnecessary tripping under normal operational surges.
    Widely used in lighting circuits, appliance connections and control panels, our Single Pole MCBs feature a compact build, high breaking capacity and a fast-acting trip mechanism, making them ideal for reliable everyday circuit protection.

  • Double Pole MCBs (C Series): Available in 6A, 10A, 16A and 32A variants, our Double Pole MCBs provide simultaneous disconnection of both phase and neutral, making them an excellent choice for applications that require added safety or dual-line control. These are essential for sub-distribution boards, motor protection and situations where equipment must be completely isolated for maintenance.
    Each of our Double Pole breaker ensures enhanced arc suppression, reliable performance under variable loads and long operational life.

  • Isolator DP (Heavy-Duty Switching): Rated at 40A and 63A, our Double Pole Isolators are built for robust disconnection in larger systems. These are not tripping devices but serve as reliable isolators for maintenance and service operations. Designed for high-load circuits, they maintain electrical integrity even after repeated switching operations.
    With reinforced terminals, secure mounting and a clean break mechanism, our isolators form the backbone of safe system shutdowns and sectional isolations.

  • Main Switch (Central Control with High Capacity): Available in 16A and 32A configurations, the Main Switch range provides a central disconnection mechanism for residential and commercial boards. These devices are crafted for high thermal endurance and mechanical longevity, ensuring quick and reliable shutoff when needed most.
    Ideal for main incomer control in consumer units, our switches combine ease of use with rugged construction for dependable circuit management.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. What is the difference between a Single Pole and Double Pole MCB?

A Single Pole (SP) MCB interrupts only the live wire, commonly used in household applications. A Double Pole (DP) MCB disconnects both live and neutral wires, offering enhanced protection and typically used in higher load or industrial applications.

Q2. What makes GeeCab MCBs different from other brands?

GeeCab MCBs are built with high-grade polymers, precision components and tested mechanisms that ensure superior performance even in fluctuating power conditions. With long mechanical life, better arc-quenching ability and BIS compliance, our products provide greater safety and long-term value.
