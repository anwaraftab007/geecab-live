Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. How do I select the right size clip or tie for my wiring project?

Evaluate the cable diameter and the number of cables in the bundle. For thin single wires, smaller clips (4mm to 7mm) and narrow ties (1.8mm width) work best. For heavy industrial cables or bundles, opt for Gutka clips (up to 40mm) and wider, longer nylon ties for better strength.

Q2. Can your plastic clips and nylon ties be used outdoors in harsh conditions?

Yes. All our products are designed with UV resistance and weatherproof features, making them suitable for outdoor installations exposed to sun, rain and humidity typical in regions like Assam and West Bengal.

Q3. How does using quality clips and ties improve installation safety?

Proper clips prevent cables from sagging or rubbing against sharp edges, reducing insulation damage. Non-metallic materials avoid conduction risks and robust ties secure bundles, minimizing hazards and improving system longevity.

Q4. Do you supply in bulk for government or infrastructure projects?

Absolutely. GeeCab works closely with contractors, OEMs and infrastructure developers, supplying bulk quantities at competitive pricing. Our logistics and quality assurance systems are equipped to handle large-scale procurement requirements with consistency and reliability.

Q5. Are your clips suitable for data cables or fiber lines?

Yes. Smaller sizes like 4mm, 5mm and 6mm clips are ideal for securing data cables, LAN wires or optical fibers. The smooth finish and precise sizing reduce the risk of insulation damage or signal interference during installation.
