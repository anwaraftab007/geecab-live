Applications of GeeCab PVC Insulation Tapes

Our PVC Electric Tapes are versatile, making them suitable for a wide range of sectors and projects:

  • Residential Use: Secure household wiring, fix minor faults or insulate electrical appliances safely and easily.

  • Commercial Installations: Perfect for malls, schools, offices and hospitals where reliable insulation is essential.

  • Industrial Applications: Resistant to heat and abrasion, ideal for heavy machinery, control panels and high-load systems.

  • Outdoor & Agricultural Setups: Weatherproof insulation for submersible pump wiring, motor repairs and outdoor installations.

  • Automotive & DIY Projects: Widely used for automotive wiring and general electrical repairs by professionals and hobbyists.
