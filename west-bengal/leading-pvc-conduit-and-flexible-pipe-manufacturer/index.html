Secure. Durable. Made for Bengal.

In today’s fast-paced infrastructure and electrical landscape, choosing the right conduit system is critical to ensuring long-term safety and performance. At GeeCab, we understand this need deeply. Backed by over 25 years of industry experience, Gananayak Cable Industries has established itself as a leading manufacturer and supplier of PVC conduit and flexible pipes in West Bengal, trusted by electricians, developers and contractors across residential, commercial and industrial sectors.

Whether you’re building a smart home in Kolkata, setting up a warehouse in Durgapur, or managing rural electrification projects in North Bengal, GeeCab delivers durable, fire-retardant and easy-to-install conduit solutions that meet the highest safety and performance standards.

Why Choose GeeCab for PVC Conduit & Flexible Pipes?

We’re more than just a cable manufacturer—we’re your infrastructure partner. Here's why thousands across West Bengal and Eastern India rely on our PVC conduit systems:

  • ISO Certified Manufacturing: All our conduit pipes are manufactured in ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 14001:2015 certified facilities, ensuring consistency, safety and quality at every step.


  • High-Grade Virgin PVC: We use only virgin, fire-retardant PVC compounds to produce pipes that are rigid, strong and built for longevity—ideal for demanding Indian environments.


  • Fire-Resistant & Self-Extinguishing: Our pipes help contain and prevent the spread of fire, offering critical protection in residential buildings, commercial complexes and industrial units.


  • Corrosion & Weather Resistant: Unlike metal conduits, GeeCab PVC pipes are immune to rust and oxidation, making them perfect for humid, coastal and monsoon-prone regions of West Bengal.


  • Smooth Finish for Easy Installation: The inner surface of every pipe is engineered for smooth wire pulling, reducing friction and preventing insulation damage during installation.


  • UV-Stable and Impact-Resistant: Suitable for indoor and outdoor applications, our pipes perform reliably even under harsh sunlight or mechanical stress.

Flexible Pipes for Dynamic Electrical Needs

In addition to rigid conduits, GeeCab offers a specialized range of Flexible PVC Pipes designed for installations where manoeuvrability and ease of routing are key. These pipes are widely used in industries, modular setups and mobile electrical systems.

Applications of Flexible Pipes

  • Modular and prefabricated buildings

  • Temporary installations and setups

  • Equipment cabling in commercial kitchens, labs and industries

  • Residential false ceilings and concealed wiring systems

Applications Across Sectors

GeeCab PVC conduit and flexible pipes are engineered for modern electrical infrastructure across a wide range of sectors:

  • Residential Wiring: Safe, neat and concealed wiring for homes, apartments and housing complexes.

  • Commercial Projects: Ideal for schools, hospitals, malls and office spaces needing organized and secure cabling systems.

  • Industrial Use: Withstand high loads, vibrations and temperature variations in factories and processing units.

  • Agricultural & Rural Installations: Protect wiring for motors, pumps and control panels in challenging outdoor conditions.
