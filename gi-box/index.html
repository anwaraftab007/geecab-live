Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. What makes GeeCab GI Boxes better than regular modular boxes in the market?

GeeCab uses premium-grade galvanized iron and advanced fabrication techniques to ensure strength, corrosion resistance and precision. Our GI Boxes are designed for professional-grade installations where safety, durability and ease of use are essential.

Q2. Are your GI Boxes compatible with all modular switch brands?

Yes, our GI Modular Boxes follow industry-standard dimensions and are compatible with all leading brands of modular switches and plates. This ensures complete flexibility in your choice of electrical fittings.

Q3. Can your GI Boxes be used in coastal or humid areas?

Absolutely. The galvanized coating on our boxes offers excellent resistance to moisture and corrosion, making them ideal for coastal cities, high-humidity areas and outdoor installations.

Q4. Do you supply to wholesalers and infrastructure contractors?

Yes, GeeCab has a strong supply chain network across India and Bhutan. We work with dealers, wholesalers and infrastructure contractors, providing bulk pricing and on-time delivery for all project sizes.
