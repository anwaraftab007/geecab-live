Best Wire and Cables Manufacturer in Bihar

GeeCab is one of the top manufacturers of Wires and Cables in Bihar. We are an ISO 9001: 2015 & ISO 14001: 2015 certified company and one stop solution for electrical appliances in India. We deal in wide range of electrical appliances that include Electric Meters, PVC Conduit Pipes, MCB Box, Extension Cords, PVC Tapes, Nano Boxes etc. We are known for providing products that are reliable and robust.

We use high standards and advanced technology to develop in-demand wires and cables for residential, commercial, and industrial purposes. Our product range includes Copper Wires, Single and Multicore cables, CO-AXIAL cables and CCTV Cables, PVC Submersible Cables, CAT-5 cables and more.

Being one of the best wires and cables manufacturer, GeeCab’s Electrical Wires are flexible and fire resistant, which provides 100% conductivity and protection from any circuit break outs. Our range of wires and cables is suitably designed for two-way communication.

We support the ‘Make in India’ campaign and produce ISI marked products. Superior quality and hassle free performance, tested by our customers, have made GeeCab one of the major market leaders. Owing to the expertise and experience of our company’s workforce, we are a leading name among manufacturers of the wide range of meter solutions in Arunachal Pradesh. We are committed to providing the best range of electric meters to our customers.
