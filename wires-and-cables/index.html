Innovation Rooted in Expertise

With more than two decades of manufacturing excellence, GeeCab has mastered the art of combining technical precision with material integrity. Every strand is drawn, annealed and insulated under strict quality supervision to ensure the highest electrical conductivity and mechanical strength.

The engineering process behind GeeCab’s products reflects an unwavering commitment to safety, efficiency and longevity. Each cable undergoes multiple tests for insulation resistance, voltage stability and temperature endurance before leaving the production floor. This meticulous attention to detail has positioned GeeCab among the best wire and cable manufacturers in India, trusted by electricians, engineers and large-scale developers alike.

Engineered for Indian Conditions

India’s climate ranges from humid coastal regions to dry and arid zones, creating some of the most demanding environments for electrical performance. GeeCab Wires and Cables are carefully designed to operate reliably across these varied conditions, ensuring stability even during voltage fluctuations, high moisture levels and prolonged operational use.

Each wire is protected with heat-resistant and flame-retardant insulation compounds that guard against wear, enhance durability and extend service life. The high-quality copper and aluminium conductors deliver excellent conductivity, maintaining steady power flow even during intensive load conditions.

From modern city networks to agricultural irrigation systems and residential connections, GeeCab products ensure consistent reliability, superior energy efficiency and complete safety at every point of use.

A Complete Range of Wiring Solutions

GeeCab offers a complete range of wires and cables engineered for strength, flexibility and longevity. Each category serves a specific application while maintaining uniform standards of quality and performance.

  • Flexible Multi-Strand Wires: Designed for effortless installation and high flexibility, GeeCab Flexible Multi-Strand Wires combine strength and conductivity in one design. The fine copper strands allow effortless bending while maintaining full electrical efficiency. Each roll is insulated with high-grade PVC that resists heat, abrasion and voltage stress.

    Ideal for domestic circuits, control panels and appliances, our wires ensure steady power flow, minimal energy loss and long-term reliability, making them the preferred choice for professionals.

  • Multi-Core Round Cables: Precision and performance define GeeCab Multi-Core Round Cables. Each cable contains multiple insulated conductors enclosed in a robust outer sheath designed to handle continuous load and mechanical stress.

    Used in control systems, heavy machinery and power transmission setups, they guarantee safe, efficient current distribution. With their flexible structure and strong insulation, they represent the perfect balance of durability and dependability.

  • Twin Flat Cables (Service Cables): Built for smooth and efficient power transmission, GeeCab Twin Flat Cables deliver reliability in every installation. Their flat configuration enables easy routing, while their high-strength insulation ensures safety in both indoor and outdoor applications.

    They are ideal for residential and commercial wiring, providing stable voltage flow, reduced energy loss and long-lasting performance even under varying environmental conditions.

  • Submersible Flat Cables: Deep beneath the surface, where performance cannot fail, GeeCab Submersible Flat Cables power water pumps and borewells with unmatched reliability. Designed to resist water, heat and pressure, they maintain insulation integrity even under submerged conditions.

    Their advanced PVC composition ensures moisture protection and flexibility while preventing cracking or corrosion. GeeCab’s engineering precision guarantees consistent current flow, making them the trusted choice for agriculture, irrigation and industrial use.

  • Aluminium Wires: When strength and economy must work together, GeeCab Aluminium Wires offer an ideal solution. Crafted from high-purity aluminium, they combine lightweight structure with excellent conductivity, reducing transmission losses while maintaining tensile strength.

    Perfect for overhead lines, distribution panels and earthing systems, our wires deliver stability, corrosion resistance and long operational life, even in outdoor installations.

  • House Wires: Safety and reliability define every GeeCab House Wire. Manufactured using high-quality copper and advanced flame-retardant insulation, they provide secure and efficient power for residential and commercial spaces.

    Their insulation resists high temperatures and electrical surges, ensuring peace of mind and consistent voltage supply. Each wire undergoes multiple safety tests to guarantee dependable performance and protection in everyday use.

  • Coaxial Wires: For flawless transmission and signal clarity, GeeCab Coaxial Wires are engineered with precision. Dual shielding ensures low interference, maintaining strong signal quality across long distances.

    Ideal for televisions, broadband and satellite communication, they ensure clear connectivity, minimal distortion and reliable performance, making them the backbone of modern communication infrastructure.

  • Cat6 Cables (Ethernet): In an age of high-speed data transfer, GeeCab Cat6 Cables set the benchmark for connectivity. Designed for Gigabit Ethernet applications, they deliver exceptional bandwidth and minimal signal loss, ensuring uninterrupted performance for offices, data centers and smart homes.

    Each pair of conductors is twisted and insulated for maximum data integrity and reduced crosstalk, providing fast, secure and efficient network connections in every environment.

  • CCTV Wires: Dependable security starts with reliable wiring. GeeCab CCTV Wires ensure uninterrupted video transmission and power delivery for surveillance systems. Their high-quality copper conductors and double shielding eliminate signal drop and interference.

    By integrating power and video within a single sheath, installation becomes faster and neater. They are trusted by professionals who demand crystal-clear monitoring, durability and consistent performance.
