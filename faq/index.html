What products does GeeCab manufacture and supply?

GeeCab offers a wide range of electrical products including wires & cables, PVC conduit pipes, electric meters, gang/nano & MCB boxes, extension cords and PVC electrical tapes. All products are made in India with a focus on quality, durability and safety, catering to residential, commercial and industrial applications.

Are GeeCab's products compliant with Indian safety and quality standards?

Absolutely. All GeeCab products are manufactured under ISO-certified processes (ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 14001:2015) and meet relevant Indian safety and quality standards. This ensures distributors/retailers receive reliable, certified products that meet regulatory requirements for electrical installations.

How can wholesalers partner with GeeCab for bulk purchases?

Wholesalers interested in partnering with GeeCab can contact our sales team via phone or email. We offer competitive pricing, flexible order quantities, and timely deliveries. Our dedicated support ensures seamless order processing and access to the latest products in wires, cables, electric meters, and more.

Can distributors get exclusive territory rights for GeeCab products?

Exclusive distribution rights depend on market potential and business volume. Interested wholesalers can discuss possibilities directly with our sales team to explore territory exclusivity and partnership terms tailored to support business growth in West Bengal and beyond.

What warranty and after-sales support do GeeCab products come with?

GeeCab products come with manufacturer warranties that vary by product category, covering manufacturing defects and performance issues. Our customer service team provides prompt after-sales support, including troubleshooting, replacements and technical assistance to ensure complete satisfaction.

How can retailers stock GeeCab products for their stores?

Retailers can connect with GeeCab through our official sales channels or authorized distributors. We facilitate easy access to our product range with minimum order quantities suited for retail stocking, ensuring a consistent supply of high-quality wires, cables, and electrical accessories.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

The MOQ varies depending on the product category. Please contact our sales department for detailed MOQ information tailored to your order requirements.

How frequently do you launch new products or updates?

We continually innovate and expand our product range to meet market demands. Wholesalers/Retailers are kept updated through product catalogues, and direct communications whenever new product lines or upgrades are launched.

What makes GeeCab products stand out in the electrical accessories market?

GeeCab is known for its commitment to quality, affordability, and innovation. Our products undergo stringent quality checks and use high-grade materials. Combined with excellent customer service and an extensive distribution network, GeeCab is a trusted brand across West Bengal and Eastern India.

How can I get a product catalogue or price list?

You can request the latest product catalogue and price list by contacting our sales team via phone or email, or download it directly from the official GeeCab website for your convenience.

What safety precautions should be followed when using GeeCab electrical accessories?

When using GeeCab electrical accessories like extension cords, MCB boxes, or gang boxes, always follow recommended installation guidelines and ensure devices are used within their rated capacities. Avoid exposure to excessive moisture or heat, and consult a qualified electrician for installation to prevent hazards and ensure optimal performance.

How does GeeCab ensure timely delivery to its distributor network across West Bengal and beyond?

GeeCab maintains a robust supply chain and inventory management system to ensure timely dispatches. We partner with reliable logistics providers for safe and prompt delivery. Also, real-time order tracking and efficient inventory management help minimize delays, ensuring distributors receive products promptly across West Bengal and other regions.
