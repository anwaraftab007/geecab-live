Comprehensive Range of MCB Distribution Boards

GeeCab offers an extensive range of MCB Distribution Boards in ABS and Metal enclosures, available in multiple configurations to meet diverse installation needs:

  • ABS Distribution Boards: Available in 4 Way, 6 Way, 8 Way and 12 Way options, these boards are lightweight, corrosion-resistant and ideal for modern residential or office applications where aesthetics meet functionality.

  • Metal Distribution Boards: Offered in 4 Way, 6 Way, 8 Way, 10 Way, 12 Way and 16 Way configurations, these are built for high durability, strength and safety. Perfect for industrial, commercial and infrastructure use, they deliver exceptional mechanical protection and heat resistance.

Each unit is thoroughly tested for dielectric strength, insulation resistance, current carrying capacity and temperature rise to ensure absolute reliability. The use of flame-retardant materials, corrosion-proof hardware and secure locking mechanisms adds an extra layer of protection, ensuring that every connection remains safe and stable under continuous load.
