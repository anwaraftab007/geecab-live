About Us

Powering Progress with Precision and Purpose

With over 25 years of experience, GeeCab has grown from a regional manufacturer into one of Eastern Indiaâ€™s trusted names in electrical solutions. Headquartered in Kolkata and proudly serving West Bengal, Odisha, Bihar, Jharkhand, Assam, Nagaland, and Bhutan, we offer a comprehensive range of products including wires and cables, PVC conduit and flexible pipes, gang boxes, extension cords, PVC tapes, electric meters, and wall lights.

All our products are manufactured under ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 14001:2015 certified systems, ensuring strict quality control, environmental compliance, and long-term performance. Our deep understanding of local infrastructure challenges, combined with strong relationships with channel partners and on-ground professionals, allows us to deliver tailored, dependable solutions for residential, agricultural, commercial, and industrial applications.

Driven by innovation, customer trust, and service excellence, GeeCab continues to power homes, businesses, and infrastructure projects with products engineered for safety, durability, and future-ready performance.

Our Mission

At GeeCab, our mission is to deliver safe, high-quality electrical products that meet the real-world needs of our customers. We are committed to building lasting relationships by offering reliable wires, cables, and accessories backed by innovation, certified manufacturing, and strong regional service across India and Bhutan.

Our vision is to be a recognized leader in electrical solutions by creating products that are safe, modern, and environmentally responsible. We strive to enhance everyday living and industrial performance through consistent quality, customer satisfaction, and a deep understanding of regional infrastructure and power distribution needs.

Our Values

We value trust, quality, innovation, and responsibility in everything we do. From our manufacturing processes to our customer relationships, we uphold the highest standards. Our team works with integrity to deliver ISO-certified products that serve the growing needs of homes, businesses, and industries across India and Bhutan.

GeeCab

Why Choose Us?

At GeeCab, we are committed to delivering electrical solutions that meet the highest standards of safety, performance, and reliability. Our products are trusted by professionals and preferred across residential, commercial, industrial, and agricultural sectors throughout India.

Cutting Edge Technology

We integrate the most advanced technologies into our manufacturing processes. Our product range, including fire-retardant wires, digital electric meters, and other smart electrical components, is designed to meet the evolving demands of modern infrastructure.

Environmentally Responsible Manufacturing

Our commitment to the environment is reflected in our use of natural and recyclable materials. Every product is manufactured under ISO 14001:2015 environmental standards, helping reduce environmental impact without compromising quality.

Consistent Product Quality

Every GeeCab product is manufactured under ISO 9001:2015 certified systems, ensuring consistency, durability, and compliance with national and international safety standards. From PVC conduit pipes to extension cords, our products are rigorously tested for reliability.

Fire-Resistant Products

Safety is non-negotiable. Our fire-retardant cables and accessories are specifically designed to limit the spread of flames and reduce toxic gas emissions during fire incidents, offering maximum protection in critical environments.
