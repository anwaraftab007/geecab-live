Durable Protection for Every Electrical Installation

Secure and efficient electrical networks begin with durable conduits. GeeCab, with over 25 years of electrical expertise, presents its premium line of PVC Conduit Pipes, crafted for safety, reliability and long-lasting performance. Engineered for residential, commercial and industrial applications, our conduits offer seamless protection for wiring systems across diverse environments.

Available in thicknesses of 1.0 mm, 1.5 mm and 2.0 mm and diameters of 20 mm, 25 mm and 32 mm, GeeCab PVC Conduit Pipes cater to diverse electrical requirements. Designed to withstand Light, Medium and Heavy Mechanical Stress, the conduits ensure reliable protection for household wiring, commercial installations and high-demand industrial setups. Compliant with IS 9537 Part 3 and IS 14927 Part 2, our pipes maintain consistent performance under challenging Indian conditions.

Precision Engineering for Safety and Durability

Electrical infrastructure requires components that perform under stress while maintaining structural integrity. GeeCab PVC Conduit Pipes are manufactured with precision, using high-quality PVC compounds that deliver impact resistance, tensile strength and dimensional stability. Each pipe undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure smooth installation, consistent wall thickness and exceptional rigidity, guaranteeing dependable protection for wires over decades.

The careful selection of raw materials ensures conduits resist moisture, temperature changes and mechanical strain. This attention to detail minimizes the risk of cracks, deformation or electrical hazards, offering electricians and installers peace of mind during and after installation.

Versatile Sizes and Mechanical Grades

Understanding the varying demands of electrical projects, GeeCab offers PVC Conduit Pipes in multiple sizes and mechanical grades.

  • 20 mm, 25 mm and 32 mm diameters accommodate small, medium and large cabling requirements.

  • Light Mechanical Stress (LMS) pipes are ideal for residential circuits and low-stress environments.

  • Medium Mechanical Stress (MMS) pipes offer robust performance for commercial setups with moderate mechanical load.

  • Heavy Mechanical Stress (HMS) pipes provide exceptional durability for industrial applications exposed to high stress or frequent handling.

This wide range ensures that every electrical installation is equipped with conduits tailored to its mechanical demands, enhancing durability and reliability.
